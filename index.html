Who am i?


My name is Gian. I am 26 years old and i am from Italy. I can speak Italian, English and a little bit of Vietnamese. I hold a bachelor degree in Material Science, a Tefl certificate and i am currently working as a teacher of English as a second language.

I have been teaching English for 2 years at Maple Bear International kindergarten in Vietnam. I am passionate about this job. I enjoy the kids's company and i like to think that they also enjoy having me as a teacher. Due to covid19, in September 2021 i switched to virtual classes.

I love travelling. Soon after my graduation, i left for Australia where i have worked and travelled for 2 years. This experience has given me much more than i expected.
